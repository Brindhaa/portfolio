User Journey

The user journey with Drops is designed to be engaging and rewarding from start to finish. From the moment users onboard, theyre seamlessly guided through a series of intuitive screens that build excitement and clarity.

  • Engaging Onboarding: Captivates users with an intuitive and friendly introduction.
  • Clear Guidance: Structured flow with easy-to-follow instructions.
  • Interactive Quests: Keeps users motivated with dynamic, gamified challenges.
  • Seamless Sign-In: Smooth Google sign-in integration after exploring community.

Initially, an eligibility criteria screen was included in the onboarding process. Users had to meet specific requirements before proceeding.

II. Iterations - Identified Issue: Confusing Eligibility Criteria

The app originally featured challenges as a way to engage users and keep them active.

Data showed that the term "challenge" was perceived as too difficult or time-consuming, leading to higher drop-off rates.

  • 1. Replaced the term "challenges" with "quests" and redesigned the flow to make these tasks feel more approachable and rewarding.
  • 2. This change led to a noticeable improvement in user engagement and a higher conversion rate. Users were more inclined to complete quests, contributing to the app's overall success.
